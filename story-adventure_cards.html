Adventure Cards

Adventure Cards are sold in stores within the UK and offered through partnerships with corporate clients as reward incentives for employees good work. They are a physical representation of the Geronigo network.

Story goals:

  1. Create a recognisable brand identity The concept was to establish a credible brand logo with supporting digital and print marketing merchandise with a smaller supporting website site.
  2. Focus on depth of Activity range, not on converting sales The concept was to establish a credible brand logo with supporting digital and print marketing merchandise with a smaller supporting website site.

Story skills:

Branding There are a variety of different user types; Explorers, activity focussed, event planners and more.

Screen flow Search engine links create different points of entry in the user journey.

Responsive UI Better vendor filtering is needed; Category, activity, location, price range, age, rating

Information architecture Page content needs to match screen purpose, funnel the user journey and also link to the wider network.
