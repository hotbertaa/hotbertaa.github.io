A prototype for Geronigo's event planner

Geronigo's event planner is a space for event organisers to plan and complete their event post enquiry / booking. Event participants can be invited and prompted for rsvp status. Individual guest payments are available, in addition to information on the event and a chat area.

The event planner is a valuable tool for managing booking payments & in retaining general event enquiries. It also helps extend Geronigo's reach beyond the event organiser; as one event could produce from 1 to 10's of new customers, extending the brands awareness and creating new revenue streams.

RezBot rezbot.net

A Suite of products for activity suppliers

The RezBot suite is a group of products tailored to the needs of activity operators. Ranging from availability calendars with online booking features, automated voucher purchase and distribution, through to visitor check in with electronic signature.

The RezBot suite encourages activity operators to join the Geronigo network in addition to offering independant products that help their business.

A prototype for RezBot's mobile bookings manager

RezBot's bookings manager is its leading platform, responsible for processing enquiries and bookings for Geronigo in addition to various other event manager's and activity operator's.

The Booking manager processes over 1,000,000 bookings a year for the Geronigo brand alone. Allowing access to the system for individual use by activity operator's is a valuable means of recruiting activity venues to the Geronigo Network.

My design philosophy Understanding, Inspiration, Simplicity

Understand the User

Good design adapts to the users needs. A users requirements may differ from what is classed as 'looking good'.

avoid design trends, unless a user demands it.

Inspire Yourself

Good design isn't a blank canvas. It comes from having a broad sample of inspiration as a start point.

reference competition, use design resources.

Simplify Needs

Good design knows when to remove something. Refine a design by taking away elements to improve the overall design.

form follows function, performance is crucial.

Self Study Projects

The following are quick projects I've created to develop my skills with certain prototyping apps, particular focus has been on animation.

Football Exploration

Football is a passion of mine and combined with the availability of high quality imagery I thought it would make an interesting prototype to further my understanding of InVision, its responsive layout and animation capabilities.

I liked the percentage layout within InVision and found the animation to be on a par with Principle, but ultimately I felt the responsive layout was lacking for multi column layouts and its cloud hosting of animations really lagged. I switched back to sketch and principle to complete the project.

A conceptual design for Geronigo's affiliate network website.

Geronigo's affiliate network is a B2B focussed site used to recruit affiliate partners to promote Geronigo's activities on there own websites, gaining commission based on the number of tracked visitors and visitors who subsiquently book through the Geronigo website.

The prototype was a learning tool to aquire skills to transition from Sketch to Figma and to gain a greater understanding of Principles driver function.
