Event Planner | Case Study

A platform for activity operators to manage bookings

Geronigo's event planner is a space for event organisers to plan and complete their event post enquiry / booking. Event participants can be invited and prompted for rsvp status. Individual guest payments are available, in addition to information on the event and a chat area.

The event planner is a valuable tool for managing booking payments & in retaining general event enquiries. It also helps extend Geronigo's reach beyond the event organiser; as one event could produce from 1 to 10's of new customers, extending the brands awareness and creating new revenue streams.
