Every Adventure...

For a large part of my career I sold activities. From a beginning of 10 activities at approximately 500 locations in the UK; to over 100 activities at 10,000s of locations worldwide.

How do you display so much choice?

The key points in the user journey:

  1. Make a first impression
  2. Prioritise search filtering: activity, location, price range, age, rating... more or less? it needed testing...
  3. Transition to venue information
  4. Encourage event enquiries and bookings: users change to subscribers and extend their product journey.
