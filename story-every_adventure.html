User Analysis & Personas

Working with sales management to analysis enquiry and sales data, 3 primary users were created.

Activity FocussedHappy Path

Visits with an activity in mind and only wishes to book that specific activity based on locality, price and date availability.

User needs:

  • SEO friendly activity/location pages filtering users away from main adventure list.
  • High screen visibility of 5~7 nearest vendor; easy price, restriction comparison
  • Vendor sites need to promote easy booking online
Party Organisers

Organising a work outing, stag party or hen party. They have some idea of the activity they want to experience but are open to suggestion.

User needs:

  • Specific event pages need to be easily accessible
  • Popular activities related to event need to be suggested
  • Price points per person need to be very visible
Free Spirits

Visits with the vague idea that they would like to experience something new within their free time, they are open to suggestion.

User needs:

  • The depth of activities needs to be visible.
  • First user action needs to go to adventures list; ordered by nearest activities.
  • Transition between adventures list and vendor screens needs to be fluid.

Journey mapping & funneling

With over 100 activities to search from user funneling was necessary to seperate casual browsing from activity specific.
