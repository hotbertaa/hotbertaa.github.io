Professional Information

UI/UX Designer, Front End Developer

My aim with all projects is to combine a captivating design with an intuitive experience that keeps a user engaged for a prolonged visit.

I am a UI/UX designer and front end developer with 15+ years of experience. My skill set is broad; ranging from design and protyping to front end development & graphic design. I enjoy each aspect and the variety that this brings. I work the full spectrum of the development process starting with the design and prototyping of a website/feature through to its build and implementation.

I have a through understanding of design prototyping, CSS, HTML, Javascript, PHP, in addition to a working understanding of MySQL. Experience has taught me the skills needed to work independantly and am self motivated in completing tasks. In addition I work well in a team and take the initiative to drive projects forward.

A large part of my work recently has been on website performance and seo optimisation. Inplementing the best SEO practises and working with Google insights to enhance each websites performance.

In the past year I have taken a keener interest in both Bootstrap & Googles Material platform and incorporate a lot of their guidelines and conventions into projects to maintain standardised functionality and usability.

I use the following in my daily work: Atom, Sketch, Photoshop, Bootstrap, GitHub & Illustrator. I have also used Zeplin, Balsamiq, Figma & InVision to varying extents.

I have an interest in furthering my knowledge in Adobe XD & Prott and to explore newer javascript libraries beyond jquery.
